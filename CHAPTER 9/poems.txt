The context of the movie "Manifest" revolves around the mysterious disappearance of Flight 828, which is inspired by the real-life incident of Malaysian Airlines Flight 370. The show explores the lives of the passengers who return after being missing for five years, grappling with the changes in their lives and the strange "Callings" they experience. The narrative blends elements of science fiction, fantasy, and suspense, creating a complex storyline that reflects on themes of fate and the unknown.
